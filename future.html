Future Transformation

Technology is changing the way we operate, from crime to our daily lives. Personally, my opinion regarding the future with technology is mixed. We will reach a point where everything will be so technologically advanced and that there will be the creation of more serious undetectable crimes.

The most challenging thing I think will be is the regulation of these new technologies. Politics and the well-being of citizen will clash causing problems.

Listed are a few things that I am optimistic about the future:

  1. Creation of new jobs
    • Even though some things will be automated causing a loss of jobs regarding low levels, I hope that there will be regulations in place to prevent organizations from completely automating a business rather than employing humans.
  2. Better Privacy
    • Privacy can be a disadvantage but it also can be an advantage, I'm hoping for a future where our privacy will be respected and we will have true freedom of expression
  3. Economy Booster
    • New technologies equal to new markets. The creation of new businesses will add value to a country's economy.

However, this world is only controlled by greed, corruption and politics. Therefore, some things may go very wrong. Fortunately, I have positive hopes regarding moving to Mars.
